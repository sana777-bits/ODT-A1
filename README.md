# ODT-A1
The Pac-Man Arcade is a physical tabletop challenge that blends retro charm with modern interactive design. Players use a handmade paper catapult to aim a tiny ball at a stationary target basket while a motorized decoy basket moves nearby to distract and block their shots. The game unfolds over 16 rounds of skill and precision. Each successful hit or miss is met with vibrant light animations. By the final round, a glowing ring of lights serves as a visual scoreboard, mapping out every victory and defeat in a nostalgic, high-energy battle for the high score.
